About

Aimed at providing real time analysis of the air quality index and improving environmental health, Aerify. We provide real-time data on air quality in your area, tracking pollutants such as particulate matter, ozone, and nitrogen dioxide. Easy to gauge interface allows easy access and analysis of the data with advanced visualization features. Along with information and resources for understanding air quality trends and promoting clean air practices, it hopes to provide for a greener shift.
